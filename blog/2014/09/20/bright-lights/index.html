Bright Lights, (Not Entirely) Big Data

I’ve always been interested in how we use narrative to help us cope with and understand the complex, messy systems around us. One of the most exciting things about joining the data science bootcamp at Metis has been learning how to leverage computational tools into finding narrative threads within data, that would otherwise be impossible to see.

I’m hoping this blog will detail some of these data stories—from both in and out of Metis. Eventually, I might put up some short fiction too.
